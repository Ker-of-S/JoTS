The Terminal Sciences Foundation

Founded in the aftermath of the first recorded Logoscide event, the Foundation serves as the independent governing body of the Journal. We are a coalition of theoretical physicists, logic-architects, and archivist-engineers dedicated to maintaining structural integrity in a universe prone to logical collapse.

  • Neutrality: We remain unaffiliated with any specific temporal faction.
  • Preservation: Data is archived redundantly across multiple isolated logical domains.
  • Vigilance: Continuous monitoring of theoretical boundaries to prevent cascade failures.
