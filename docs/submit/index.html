Manuscript Submission

JoTS submission page.

!

Prerequisite

Before proceeding with your submission, you must read the Author Guidelines. Submissions that do not strictly adhere to these protocols will be automatically rejected.

Format Specifications

To ensure the integrity of our databases, all online projects and documents submitted via shareable links (e.g., Google Docs, Overleaf) must be strictly set to "Read-Only" mode.

General specifications

  • Page Dimensions: Standard A4 or US Letter sizes.
  • A two-column structure is recommended, unless large objects (such as extensive data tables, schematics, or diagrams) require full-page width.
  • Margins and Indentation: Arbitrary, but authors are expected to use their best judgment to maintain a clean appearance without exaggeration.
  • Primary Text Color: Standard black.

LaTeX Submissions

  • Typography and Sizing: The default LaTeX font and size are preferred. Minor adjustments for visual comfort or specific structural needs are permitted.
  • Styling: Colors, custom designs, and additional visual elements are completely free, provided they serve the document's purpose and do not compromise data legibility.

Standard Word Processors (Microsoft Word, Google Docs, etc.)

  • Typography: Manuscripts should use IBM Plex Serif, EB Garamond, Lora, Computer Modern, or AMS fonts. We recommend maintaining a single font family throughout the document, unless a distinct environment (such as data logs or code blocks) requires differentiation.
  • Alternative Fonts: If you choose to use a font not listed above, you must verify that it is in the public domain or explicitly licensed for non-commercial use (e.g., Open Font License).
  • Mathematical Equations: The native equation font provided by your chosen word processor is perfectly acceptable.
  • Font Size
    • Article Title: 17-18 pt (Regular or Bold)
    • Author(s): 12 pt
    • Abstract Header: 10 pt (Bold)
    • Abstract Text: 9 pt
    • Section Headers: 14-15 pt (Bold)
    • Subsection Headers: 12-13 pt (Bold)
    • Main text: 10 pt
    • Footnotes: 8 pt
    • Captions: 9 pt
  • Color Usage: Arbitrary colors are permitted for headings or specific elements, but they must remain legible and preserve a professional style.
